Booking Information

We recommend booking your accommodation as early as possible, as Osaka is a popular tourist destination and hotels can fill up quickly, especially during the summer months. Most hotels offer online booking through their websites or through major booking platforms.

Please note that the conference does not have any special arrangements or discounted rates with these hotels. The information provided here is for reference purposes only to assist participants with their travel planning.
