Travelling to Osaka

By Air

Osaka is served by two major airports:

Kansai International Airport (KIX)

Kansai International Airport is the primary international gateway to the Osaka/Kansai region. Located on an artificial island in Osaka Bay, it offers flights to and from major cities worldwide.

Transportation from Kansai International Airport to the Conference Venue

  • JR Haruka Limited Express: The fastest train connection from KIX to central Osaka. Take the Haruka to Osaka Station (about 50 minutes), then transfer to the Osaka Metro Yotsubashi Line to Higobashi Station (5-minute walk to the venue).
  • Airport Limousine Bus: Direct buses run from KIX to various locations in Osaka city. The closest stop to the venue is Osaka Station, from where you can take a short taxi ride or the metro.
  • Taxi: Taxis are available at the airport but can be expensive for the long journey to central Osaka (approximately 16,000-20,000 yen).

Osaka International Airport (Itami, ITM)

Itami Airport primarily handles domestic flights within Japan, though it's closer to the city center than KIX.

Transportation from Itami Airport to the Conference Venue

  • Airport Limousine Bus: Direct buses connect Itami to Osaka Station (about 30 minutes).
  • Monorail + Train: Take the Osaka Monorail to Senri-Chuo Station, then transfer to the Midosuji Subway Line to reach central Osaka.
  • Taxi: A taxi from Itami to the conference venue takes about 30 minutes and costs approximately 4,000-6,000 yen.

Public Transportation in Osaka

Osaka has an extensive and efficient public transportation network that makes getting around the city easy.

Osaka Metro

The Osaka Metro (subway) system consists of 9 lines covering most of the city. The conference venue is a 5-minute walk from Higobashi Station on the Yotsubashi Line.

JR Trains

The JR Loop Line (Kanjo Line) circles central Osaka and connects to other JR lines. Osaka/Umeda Station is a major hub for JR trains.

Buses

Osaka has an extensive bus network, though the subway is often more convenient for visitors.

IC Cards

The ICOCA card is the rechargeable smart card for the Kansai region. It can be used on trains, subways, and buses in Osaka and surrounding areas. Other IC cards like Suica and PASMO from other regions also work in Osaka.

Getting to the Conference Venue

The Osaka University Nakanoshima Center is located at 4 Chome-3-53 Nakanoshima, Kita Ward, Osaka. The nearest stations are:

  • Higobashi Station (Yotsubashi Line): 5-minute walk
  • Yodoyabashi Station (Midosuji Line): 10-minute walk
  • Watanabebashi Station (Keihan Line): 7-minute walk
