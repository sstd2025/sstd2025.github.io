Attractions/Events in Osaka

Expo 2025

Schedule: 184 days from April 13 to October 13, 2025
Venue: Yumeshima, Osaka
Webpage: https://www.expo2025.or.jp/en/

A World Expo is an event that brings together people and innovations from around the world in an effort to address issues facing humankind on a global scale. The Osaka Expo 1970, Japan’s, and Asia’s, first ever, became a symbol of Japan’s rapid economic growth and one of the country’s most lauded events. Twenty years after the Aichi Expo 2005, Expo 2025 Osaka, Kansai will bring the international community back to Japan.

Osaka Castle

Osaka Castle is one of Japan's most famous landmarks and it played a major role in the unification of Japan during the sixteenth century. The castle grounds, which cover approximately 60,000 square meters, contain thirteen structures that have been designated as Important Cultural Assets by the Japanese government.

The main tower of Osaka Castle is now completely restored and houses a museum that documents the history of the castle and the city. The castle is surrounded by a moat and park with plum, peach, and cherry blossom trees, making it a popular spot for viewing cherry blossoms in spring.

Dotonbori

Dotonbori is one of Osaka's most popular tourist destinations, famous for its bright neon lights, extravagant signage, and food culture. The area is filled with restaurants, shops, and entertainment establishments, making it the heart of Osaka's nightlife.

The most famous landmark in Dotonbori is the Glico Running Man sign, which has become an iconic symbol of Osaka. Visitors often take photos with the sign in the background. The area is also known for its wide variety of street food and local specialties, such as takoyaki (octopus balls) and okonomiyaki (savory pancakes).

Universal Studios Japan

Universal Studios Japan (USJ) is one of four Universal Studios theme parks worldwide and the first to be built in Asia. Located in Osaka's Konohana Ward, the park features attractions based on popular films and TV shows, including Harry Potter, Jurassic Park, Spider-Man, and more.

The Wizarding World of Harry Potter is one of the park's most popular areas, featuring a recreation of Hogwarts Castle and Hogsmeade Village. Super Nintendo World, which opened in 2021, is the newest addition to the park, featuring attractions based on Nintendo games like Mario Kart and Yoshi's Adventure.
